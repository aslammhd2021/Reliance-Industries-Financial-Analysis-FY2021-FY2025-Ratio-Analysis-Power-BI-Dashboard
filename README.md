
# Reliance Industries Financial Analysis Dashboard (FY2021–FY2025)

Investor-focused financial analysis of **Reliance Industries Limited** using five years of consolidated financial statements. The project evaluates profitability, leverage, liquidity, and cash flow quality through financial ratio analysis and an interactive **Power BI dashboard**.



# Project Objective

The objective of this project is to evaluate the **financial health and long-term sustainability** of Reliance Industries Limited from an investor perspective.

The analysis focuses on determining whether the company’s growth is supported by:

* Sustainable profitability
* Efficient capital utilization
* Controlled financial leverage
* Stable liquidity position
* Strong operating cash flow



# Business Context

Large diversified corporations often demonstrate strong revenue growth. However, revenue growth alone does not guarantee financial strength or long-term stability.

Investors and analysts need to understand whether growth translates into:

* Real profitability
* Efficient capital allocation
* Sustainable debt management
* Cash-backed earnings

This project analyzes five years of financial statements to assess the company’s **overall financial resilience and investment quality**.



# Data Sources

Financial data was collected from **audited consolidated financial statements** available in Reliance Industries annual reports.

Statements used:

* Consolidated Income Statement
* Consolidated Balance Sheet
* Consolidated Cash Flow Statement

Period analyzed:

**FY2021 – FY2025**

Using primary audited sources ensures reliability and accuracy of the financial analysis.



# Data Preparation

Before analysis, the financial data was cleaned and structured in Excel.

Key steps included:

* Extracting financial statement data from annual reports
* Converting financial figures into numeric format
* Standardizing financial units across all years
* Organizing data into structured tables
* Validating financial statement consistency

Balance sheet validation:

```
Assets = Liabilities + Equity
```

This preparation ensured accurate ratio calculations and trend analysis.



# Financial Ratios Analyzed

The analysis includes key financial ratios across four major categories.

### Profitability

* Gross Profit Margin
* Net Profit Margin
* Return on Equity (ROE)
* Return on Assets (ROA)

### Liquidity

* Current Ratio
* Quick Ratio

### Solvency

* Debt-to-Equity Ratio
* Interest Coverage Ratio

### Efficiency

* Asset Turnover Ratio

### Cash Flow Quality

* Operating Cash Flow
* Free Cash Flow
* CFO to PAT ratio

These ratios help evaluate the company’s operational performance, financial stability, and risk profile.



# Dashboard Overview

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was developed to visualize financial performance trends across five years.

Dashboard sections include:

* Executive Summary
* Profitability Analysis
* Solvency & Liquidity Analysis
* Cash Flow Quality Analysis
* Financial Health Snapshot

The dashboard highlights key financial indicators and allows users to explore multi-year financial performance patterns.



# Key Insights

**Revenue Growth**

Revenue demonstrated consistent growth over the five-year period, reflecting strong expansion across business segments.

**Operating Cash Flow**

Operating cash flow increased significantly over time, indicating strong earnings quality and efficient cash generation.

**Leverage Management**

The company maintains a conservative debt structure with a low debt-to-equity ratio compared to industry peers.

**Liquidity Position**

Liquidity ratios indicate adequate short-term financial stability, although quick ratio trends highlight inventory-heavy operations.

**Capital Investment**

High capital expenditure reflects ongoing investment in infrastructure, telecom, and retail expansion.



# Conclusion

The financial analysis suggests that Reliance Industries demonstrates **strong financial resilience supported by diversified business operations**.

Key conclusions include:

* Stable profitability despite large-scale expansion
* Conservative leverage structure
* Strong operating cash generation
* Sustainable financial growth potential

Overall, the company exhibits characteristics of a **financially stable and scalable business model from an investor perspective**.
